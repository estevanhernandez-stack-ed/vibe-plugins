## Context

non-obvious facts. One to three short paragraphs.

## Decision

What we chose. Stated as a single sentence in active voice, then a brief
explanation. The decision should be specific enough that someone reading
the ADR a year from now can tell *exactly* what was chosen — not just
the general direction.

## Consequences

Include the load-bearing trade-off you accepted. If new constraints land
on downstream work, name them.

## Alternatives considered

What was on the table. Why each was rejected, in one sentence each. If
an alternative is plausibly better in a different context, name that
context — future-you may need to revisit.
